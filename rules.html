How to Play

At each step of the game you will be presented with two actions, and will have to pick one, keeping in mind the final goal is to improve the earth's challenging situation.

Final Goal

You will not be presented any wrong choice to help you understand that facing the world's challenges can be complicated and there isn't a right way to do it. You are going to have to make a decision and prioritise one thing over the other.
